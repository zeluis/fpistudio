Our core values

History

fpids started as an idea in late 2009. Starting 2010, we became a creative studio focusing on providing a high quality and cost effective alternative for video production and interactive design for small and medium size companies.

Mission

Our mission is to create innovative and strong design concepts for our clients while crafting memorable and efficient online presences for their brands and services.

Our Focus

We focus on digital content creation and online branding services by leveraging the latest industry technologies and following closely the branding and web development trends.

Our Offer

Our creative work is based on the goal of creating a memorable, evocative, and emotional experience for your customers.We are committed to find the best cost effective solutions for your project.

Technology

Be it static or dynamic, from prototype to production, we leverage the latest and reliable tech on all our projects. We are a big fan of the open source community. We believe Sharing is knowledge.

Expertize

We take a interdisciplinary approach to find clear and simple solutionsto your project needs. As a result we are able to consistently provide a full range of creative services across platforms and industries.
